# Human Relationships Are an Invisible Ledger

## Core Thesis

The relationships that make you uncomfortable aren't because the other person has "low EQ." It's because their internal ledger shows your balance at zero.

Every favor, help, and concession is recorded in the subconscious of both parties. No one shows you this book. But everyone keeps it.

---

## Three Types of Account Holders

| Type | Logic | Behavior |
|------|-------|----------|
| Reciprocal | Balanced deposits and withdrawals | Stable but shallow |
| Overdraft | Only withdraws, never deposits | Uses you then disappears |
| Savings | Deposits far more than withdrawals | Can leverage massive resources when needed |

Wealth creation isn't about hard work. It's about whether you have a balance on the right people's ledgers.

---

## How to Deposit

1. **Help during troughs, not peaks.** The interest rate on kindness during someone's lowest moment is 100x
2. **Information is the cheapest deposit.** Sharing one unknown insight costs zero, but the ledger value is enormous
3. **Don't withdraw immediately.** Let deposits sit for at least three cycles
