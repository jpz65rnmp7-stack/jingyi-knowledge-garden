# Why the Top Never Tells You the Rules of the Game

## Core Thesis

Information blockade isn't a conspiracy theory. It's the infrastructure of power structures. Every social system has two rule sets — the visible one written in handbooks, and the invisible one that governs actual power and resource distribution.

The invisible rule: **Rules only bind those whose value gap is small.** When your value is high enough, rules rewrite themselves around you.

---

## The Four Layers of Information Blockade

| Layer | How It Works | Example |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| Physical | Direct access restriction | Paywalls, private communities |
| Linguistic | Terminological barriers | Legal jargon, financial language |
| Attentional | Noise drowning signal | Hot trends, infinite scroll |
| Cognitive | Making you think you already know | "Hard work leads to success" — but nobody tells you which direction |

The most insidious layer is the cognitive one. Not blocking information — making you believe there's nothing to be blocked.

---

## Information Arbitrage

The essence of wealth creation = information gap arbitrage:
- Know what others don't → Predictive arbitrage (investing)
- Understand what others don't → Cognitive arbitrage (consulting)
- Do what others won't → Execution arbitrage (entrepreneurship)

---

> The value of information isn't in the information itself. It's in how many people don't have it yet.
